Constitution

Semba AI is a Company Limited by Guarantee and has no Shareholders; it operates as an oversight body operating at arm's length within the Cognitif Group, and acts as an independent Data Custodian.

The company has been formally constituted to protect information, respect privacy and support responsible data stewardship, and its governance framework follows internationally recognised standards, including ISO/IEC 27001, ISO/IEC 27701 and ISO/IEC 42001, providing a structured path towards independent certification.

Governance framework

Aligned to internationally recognised standards

  • ISO/IEC 27001

    Information Security

    Management of information security risk across the custodianship of the dataset.

  • ISO/IEC 27701

    Privacy Information

    Extension of the security management system to privacy-specific controls.

  • ISO/IEC 42001

    AI Management Systems

    Governance of AI-related risk, oversight and responsible stewardship.
